What is AaaS

Not software. Not a project.
A team subscription.

AI Agents as a Service means you subscribe to a dedicated team of AI specialists — the way you subscribe to Salesforce, but instead of a CRM, you get agents that actually do the work.

01

Agents, not tools

A tool waits for you to use it. An agent works autonomously — reading inputs, making decisions, executing tasks, and reporting back. The difference is whether your business runs without you or not.

02

Subscribed, not built

Traditional AI projects take 6–12 months and cost AED 200K+. With AaaS, your agents are live in 3 weeks for a fraction of the cost — and they improve every month automatically.

03

Specialists, not generalists

Every agent is a deep specialist. A lead qualifier only qualifies and routes leads. A report writer only generates performance reports. Depth over breadth — every time.

04

Self-improving by design

Every month, your agents get smarter. Agent Lab — our meta-agent — scrapes AI research daily, reads your configs, and upgrades them automatically. Your stack evolves while you sleep.

FAQ

Questions we always get.

If something is not answered here, reach out directly. The team responds same day.

What exactly is Agents-as-a-Service?+
AaaS means you subscribe to a team of AI specialist agents monthly — no large upfront build cost. Each agent is custom-built for your business, deployed to your channels, and improves automatically over time.
How long does it take to go live?+
3 weeks from signed agreement to live agents. Week 1 is discovery and design. Week 2 is build and testing. Week 3 is deployment and staff training.
Do we need to change our existing tools?+
No. We integrate with what you already use — WhatsApp Business, your calendar, CRM, email, project management tools, and databases. We do not replace your stack. We connect agents to it.
Do we need a technical team or IT department?+
No. We handle everything — design, integration, testing, deployment, and maintenance. Your staff attend a 2-hour training session when we go live. No technical knowledge required.
What does "self-improving" actually mean?+
Every night, Agent Lab scrapes 6 AI research sources, reads all your agent configurations, and generates improvement recommendations. Small fixes are applied automatically. Bigger changes are sent to you via WhatsApp for approval. Your agents in month 6 are materially smarter than on day one.
What happens if an agent makes a mistake?+
Every agent has a human escalation path. When something falls outside its confidence threshold, it flags the case to your team with full context. All interactions are logged and auditable.
What is the ROI? When does it pay for itself?+
Most businesses recover the cost within the first 2–3 weeks through staff time savings alone — typically 15–25 hours per week. A single team member doing repetitive admin costs AED 8,000 – 15,000 per month. The agents handle that work for a fraction of the cost.
Is there a minimum contract length?+
We start with a 3-month pilot. After that, you move to month-to-month. No lock-ins beyond the initial pilot.
